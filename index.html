About

The team
behind the work

I'm Ryan Symes — a graphic designer based in Ottawa, Ontario, studying at Algonquin College and obsessed with building brands that hit hard. I specialise in sports design, motion graphics, and brand identity — work that lives at the intersection of high-energy culture and sharp visual thinking.

What makes us different is simple — we're family. Sean Symes leads creative direction and Gabe Symes drives talent and relationships. We've grown up competing, pushing each other, and holding each other to a higher standard every single day. That's not something you can fake, and it shows in everything we put out. Family is the foundation — that's what makes us so strong.
