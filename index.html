About

Design that
speaks volumes

Based in Ottawa, Ontario, Ryan Symes is a graphic designer with over 4 years of experience translating complex ideas into visual languages that stick. He's built brands for independent studios and global companies alike, always starting with the same question — what do you want people to feel?

A graduate of Algonquin College, Ryan's obsession with sports design and motion graphics runs deep — his work lives at the intersection of high-energy branding and intuitive creativity.
